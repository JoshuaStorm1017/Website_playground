Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

We offer a comprehensive range of services including forms, full color commercial printing, graphic design services, industrial tagging and labeling, membership cards, promotional products, and business communication packages, as well as shipping, warehousing, mailing, and fulfillment services.

How quickly can you fulfill my order?

Turnaround times vary depending on the complexity and size of your order. Standard printing jobs typically take 3-5 business days, while custom projects may require additional time. We also offer rush services for time-sensitive needs. Contact us for a specific timeline for your project.

Do you offer design services?

Yes, we have a team of experienced graphic designers who can help create or refine your print materials. From logo design to comprehensive branding packages, our creative team can assist with all your design needs.

What file formats do you accept?

We accept most common file formats including PDF, AI, EPS, PSD, INDD, JPG, PNG, and TIFF. PDF is preferred for print-ready files. For best results, all files should be high resolution (300 DPI) with fonts embedded or converted to outlines.
