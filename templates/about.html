About Consolidated Document Solutions

Home > About Us

Founded in 1966 as Consolidated Business Forms, our company has long been a distributor of professional business forms. As technology began to radically change America's offices, our products and services were re-engineered to reflect a changing marketplace. In 1999 we changed our name to Consolidated Document Solutions to more accurately reflect the full portfolio of products and services we offer.

Today, our company serves an international customer base providing forms, full color commercial printing, graphic design services, industrial tagging and labeling, membership cards, promotional products, and much more for non-profits, major corporations and industrial organizations. From complete business communication packages to shipping, warehousing, mailing and fulfillment services, Consolidated Document Solutions provides everything your business needs to communicate with employees and customers.

Our Mission

At Consolidated Document Solutions, our mission is to be your trusted partner for all business communication needs, providing exceptional quality, service, and value through innovative printing and document solutions.

Our History

For over five decades, Consolidated Document Solutions has been committed to providing quality printing and document services to businesses across various industries. What began as a business forms company has evolved into a comprehensive document solutions provider that embraces new technologies while maintaining the highest standards of quality and customer service.

Through each stage of our growth, we've maintained our commitment to personalized service. While we've expanded our capabilities and services, we've never lost sight of what matters most: helping our clients communicate effectively with their audiences through high-quality printed and digital materials.

Our Approach

At Consolidated Document Solutions, we believe in a collaborative approach to meeting our clients' needs. We take the time to understand your business objectives and work closely with you to develop solutions that achieve your goals efficiently and effectively.
